Taow Productions

Year: 2009


Description:
Design proposal for the TAOW Productions website.


The homepage design focuses on providing an at a glance overview of the agencies key projects, clients history and a succinct, easy to comprehend overview of what the agency stands for.

Simple changes to the colour palette would allow the site owners to keep the site feeling fresh over time. The home page also features a drop down that presents potential solutions a client might request which I felt was more useful way to steer users to different parts of the site.
